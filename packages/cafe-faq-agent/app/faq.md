# The Daily Grind Café — FAQ

## Opening Hours
**Q: What are your opening hours?**
A: We're open Monday to Friday, 7:30am–6pm. Saturday 8am–5pm. Closed on Sundays and public holidays.

## Location
**Q: Where are you located?**
A: We're at 12 Keong Saik Road, Singapore 089119. Nearest MRT is Outram Park (EW16/NE3/TE17), 5 min walk.

## Menu
**Q: Do you serve food or just coffee?**
A: Both! We have all-day breakfast (eggs benedict, toasts, granola bowls), pastries, and light lunch options like sandwiches and salads.

**Q: Do you have vegan or vegetarian options?**
A: Yes — we have oat milk, soy milk, and almond milk for drinks. Our granola bowl and avocado toast are fully vegan.

**Q: What coffee do you serve?**
A: We use single-origin beans from our roaster partner in Bali. We do espresso-based drinks, pour-overs, cold brew, and matcha.

**Q: Do you have seasonal specials?**
A: Yes! Check our Instagram @thedailygrindsg for monthly specials. Right now we have a Brown Sugar Hojicha Latte.

## Pricing
**Q: How much is a cup of coffee?**
A: Espresso/Americano from $5, lattes and cappuccinos from $6.50, specialty drinks from $7.50. Food from $8.

## Reservations
**Q: Can I make a reservation?**
A: We're walk-in only on weekdays. For groups of 6 or more on weekends, DM us on Instagram or WhatsApp +65 9123 4567.

## WiFi & Working
**Q: Do you have WiFi?**
A: Yes, free WiFi for all customers. Password is on your receipt. We ask that solo diners don't occupy 4-person tables during peak hours (12–2pm).

## Payment
**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Cash, all major credit/debit cards, PayNow, and GrabPay. Minimum card spend is $10.

## Takeaway & Delivery
**Q: Do you do takeaway?**
A: Yes, all items are available for takeaway. We use biodegradable cups and containers.

**Q: Do you deliver?**
A: We're on GrabFood and foodpanda. Search "The Daily Grind Keong Saik".

## Private Events
**Q: Can I book the café for a private event?**
A: Yes, we do after-hours buyouts (after 6pm). Email hello@thedailygrind.sg with your event details.

## Parking
**Q: Is there parking nearby?**
A: Yes, Ann Siang Hill Park carpark is 3 min walk. $1.20/30min on weekdays.
